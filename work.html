Process

Research

Those images shows my research for some projects. Research is a very good way to start when I got the project. Researching start from marketing, target audience and so on can help me to understand the project well and start to preparing design.

Brainstorm

Those are my brainstorm images. After research I will do the brainstorm mapping. Brainstrom can help me try to start thinking the project and collect all the ideas together.

Sketches

After done all the research and brainstrom. I will start sketching out. Sketching can help me get more and more ideas and make me more creative. I like sketch everything on the paper first, then move on to computer. That will make the steps more easier, save more time and improve work efficiency.
